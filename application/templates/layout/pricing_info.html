Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does payment work?

    Payments are handled through Stripe. None of your payment information is stored on our servers.

  • When may I cancel?

    Anytime. No questions asked. If you cancel in the middle of a your billing period, your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period.

  • If I cancel, do I get a refund?

    No. If you sign up and cancel your subscription, you will not be refunded for the remaining time in your billing period. Don't worry, you'll still have access for the remaining of the billing period.

  • Is this a subscription?

    Yes. Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing period. You can cancel at any time.

  • If I cancel in the middle of the Month, what happens?

    You will still have access to the service until the end of the billing period. You will not be refunded for the remaining time in your billing period. You will no longer be billed.

  • Why are there limits on Agents and Servers if I pay?

    There are even limits when paying because we want to ensure that the service is not abused.
